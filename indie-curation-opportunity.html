Indie Bookstores See Opportunity in Curation

Independent shops lean into staff picks, community events, and localized lists.

By Staff Reporter January 30, 2026 Chicago, IL

Programming as Placement

With limited linear feet, indies use events and book clubs to drive discovery. The result is a living planogram that reflects local tastes.

Handselling remains a key differentiator—particularly for backlist gems that algorithmic feeds rarely surface.

From Handwritten Shelftalkers to TikTok

Staff annotations and short-form video coexist as recommendation engines. The common thread: authentic voice builds trust and repeat visits.

Several stores report that community-led lists outperform generic “bestseller” signage for conversion.
