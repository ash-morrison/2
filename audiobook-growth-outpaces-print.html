Audiobook Growth Continues to Outpace Print

Listening habits normalize, expanding the funnel for narrative non-fiction and genre fiction.

By Staff Reporter January 30, 2026 Los Angeles, CA

Frictionless Discovery

Bundled subscriptions and better sampling lower barriers to trying new authors in audio. Narrator branding also drives series loyalty.

Retailers mirror the shift with expanded audiobook gifting and store-hosted listening stations during events.

Production Craft as a Differentiator

Studios emphasize casting, direction, and sound design to lift completion rates. For long-form works, pacing and chapterization matter as much as prose style.

Publishers report backlist revivals when a strong audio adaptation lands.
